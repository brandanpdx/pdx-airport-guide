PDX Security Information


All passengers must present Transportation Security Administration checkpoint staff with an airline-issued boarding pass, and passengers age 18 or older must present government-issued photo ID, such as a driver's license or passport. At the airport a boarding pass may be obtained at one of the following locations:

  • - Your airline's ticket counter
  • - Your airline's self-service ticket kiosks
  • - Your airline's curbside check-in station
